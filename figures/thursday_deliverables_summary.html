2. Curated features deliverable

We created defensible 6-10 feature sets for unemployment and industrial production, with sources and reasons for each feature.

Key files

  • docs/curated_features_unrate_indpro.md — human-readable evidence table.
  • configs/curated_feature_sets.json — machine-readable feature config.
  • src/classical_utils/curated_features.py — helper to build curated panels.
  • data/processed/track_B_unrate_curated.parquet — 767 × 9 UNRATE panel.
  • data/processed/track_B_indpro_curated.parquet — 767 × 10 INDPRO panel.

Why we did it

Track B should not look arbitrary. We now have source-backed rationale for each predictor, which is important for grading and sponsor-facing explanation.

3. Main sources used

  • Conference Board LEI: leading indicators such as manufacturing hours, initial claims, new orders, building permits, stock prices, yield spread, and expectations.
  • Federal Reserve IP methodology: industrial production uses physical product data and production-input measures such as production-worker hours.
  • Federal Reserve capacity utilization: capacity use is directly tied to industrial output intensity.
  • Stock & Watson diffusion indexes: supports macro forecasting with broad monthly panels and reduced predictor sets.
  • RBA labor-market leading indicators: supports leading-indicator logic for unemployment forecasts.
