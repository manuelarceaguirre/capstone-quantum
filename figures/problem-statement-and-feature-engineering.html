01 · Business Opportunity
Problem
Macroeconomic forecasts drive portfolio allocation, risk management, and recession preparedness across BMO Wealth Management and Capital Markets.
Classical models have known ceilings on real and nominal indicators (Konduri & Li 2024). Emerging quantum-enhanced methods may raise accuracy or reduce data / model complexity, creating a first-mover advantage if adopted early.
Core Question
If and when can Quantum Reservoir Computing (QRC) and Quantum Signature Kernels (QSK) match or beat the classical state-of-the-art for U.S. macro forecasting?
Deliverable is an assessment of near-term viability plus a recommendation on the long-term roadmap for QRC / QSK adoption.
