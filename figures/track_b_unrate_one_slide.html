Track B UNRATE Forecasts: simple persistence wins

2-year fixed input window · look-ahead h = 1, 3, 6 months · metric = MASE
Capstone baseline

Experiment setup

  • Target: future UNRATE_level[t+h]
  • Features: Track B leading indicators over trailing 24 months
  • Window: fixed 2-year history per forecast origin
  • Evaluation: shared expanding folds; overlapping test dates deduplicated
  • Metric: Mean Absolute Scaled Error; lower is better
Caveat: “2-year fixed window” here means 24-month input window, not 24-month rolling training window.

Takeaway: UNRATE levels are highly persistent. In this transparent Track B setup, richer ML models do not beat simple time-series baselines.
